About Me

I am a driven Web and UX design student who strives to create user-friendly and innovative digital products that can enhance people’s lives and experiences. I am a reliable and efficient professional who can adapt to the needs and expectations of clients and colleagues. I have applied these skills to the various web design and UX projects I've completed.

I enjoy creating digital products that are easy to use and enjoyable to interact with. I have learned how to conduct user research, create wireframes and prototypes, and test and iterate on designs through various courses and assignments. I am always curious to learn new skills and tools, and I value working with other designers and developers. My goal is to become a professional UX designer or UX researcher who can tackle complex problems and deliver great solutions for users and businesses.

In my free time, I enjoy reading books on various topics, exploring nature trails and parks, listening to music from different genres, and working on DIY projects that challenge my creativity and skills. Being able to learn something new and explore my creativity is one of my passions.
